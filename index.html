Building things that

I am a Year 2 Engineering and Product Design student at SUTD, specialising in Electrical Engineering. SUTD's project-driven curriculum has shaped how I approach engineering — I learn by building, testing, and iterating, not just in theory but in practice from the very start.

My experience spans circuit design and debugging, PCB layout, sensor and actuator integration, CAD modelling in Fusion360, and hands-on prototyping and fabrication. I am comfortable working across both the electrical and mechanical sides of a project.

Outside of engineering, I have been active in sports throughout my life — football, ultimate frisbee, and more. That discipline and drive carries directly into how I work.
